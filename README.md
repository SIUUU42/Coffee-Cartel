# Brief 1: The Local Coffee Shop ☕

Welcome to your first React Bootcamp challenge!

## Instructions

1. **Start your project:** Click the green **"Use this template"** button at the top of this repository to create your own copy. DO NOT fork it.
2. **Clone your new repo:** `git clone <your-repo-url>`
3. **Install dependencies:** Run `cd local-coffee-shop` and then `npm install` and `npm run dev` to start.
4. **Build the app:** Follow the brief requirements. Be sure to fetch the provided `/menu.json` file on mount to render your menu items!
5. **Deploy:** Deploy your `main` branch to Vercel.

## How to Submit & Get Graded

We use an automated grading bot that runs your technical tests and checks your live Vercel site for performance and accessibility.

1. Open `submission.json` in the root of your project.
2. Paste your live Vercel URL exactly like this:
   `{"url": "https://my-coffee-shop.vercel.app"}`
3. Create a new branch for your final submission: `git checkout -b submission`
4. Commit and push your changes.
5. Go to your GitHub repository and open a **Pull Request** against your own `main` branch.
6. 🚦 **Wait 2-3 minutes!** Our grading bot will automatically run your tests and Lighthouse audit, and comment your automated score (out of 50) directly on your Pull Request. I will then review your design and add up to 20 more points for a final grade out of 70!
